Game Features

Immersive Combat System

Experience fluid, high-octane battles with a combat system that faithfully recreates the heart-pounding intensity of the anime. Chain combos, unleash cursed techniques, and perform devastating finishers.

Domain Expansion

Unlock and master each character's unique Domain Expansion – the ultimate manifestation of Jujutsu sorcery. Experience breathtaking animations and turn the tide of battle with these powerful abilities.

Character Progression

Develop your sorcerer's abilities through a deep progression system. Earn experience, unlock new techniques, and customize your combat style to suit your playstyle.

Multiplayer Battles

Test your skills against other players in ranked matches or team up with friends to take on formidable special grade curses in cooperative missions.

Epic Story Campaign

Follow an original storyline set in the Jujutsu Kaisen universe, with faithful recreations of key moments from the anime and manga, plus all-new content exclusive to the game.

Fearsome Cursed Spirits

Face off against a roster of terrifying cursed spirits, from minor threats to special grade monstrosities like Mahito, Hanami, and the King of Curses himself, Ryomen Sukuna.
