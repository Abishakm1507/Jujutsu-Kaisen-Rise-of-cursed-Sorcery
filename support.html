Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? +
To create an account, click on the "Play Now" button in the top right corner of our website. You'll be directed to a registration page where you can enter your email, create a username and password. Alternatively, you can sign up using your Google, Facebook, or Discord account for a faster registration process.
How do I unlock new cursed techniques? +
New cursed techniques can be unlocked by progressing through the story mode or by achieving specific milestones in the game. Some rare techniques require completing special events or challenges. You can view your available and locked techniques in the "Techniques" section of your character profile.
I'm experiencing lag during gameplay. What can I do? +
If you're experiencing lag, try the following solutions:
- Check your internet connection and ensure you have a stable connection
- Lower your graphics settings in the game options menu
- Close other applications or browser tabs that might be using bandwidth
- Try playing during off-peak hours
- Update your graphics drivers
If problems persist, please contact our support team with details about your device specifications.
How do I reset my password? +
To reset your password, click on the "Account Settings" link in the navigation menu. On your account page, select "Security" and then "Reset Password." You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.
Is there a way to transfer my progress between devices? +
Yes! Your progress is automatically saved to your account. Simply log in with the same account credentials on any device to continue where you left off. Make sure your game is fully synced before switching devices by visiting the main menu or completing a level.
How can I report a bug or player? +
To report a bug or another player, use the "Report" feature in the game menu. For bugs, please provide as much detail as possible, including what you were doing when the bug occurred and any error messages you received. For player reports, include the player's username and a description of the incident. You can also submit reports through our contact form below.
